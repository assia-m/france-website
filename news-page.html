Top Story

Fashion loves Paris, and so does the Eiffel Tower!
From Sept 30 to Oct 7, on the occasion of the Fashion Week, the Eiffel Tower pays tribute to its close connection to artists with a spectacular lighting.

On Sept 30, at 9 pm, a 6 min light show will celebrate the opening of the event and unveil the light message "Fashion likes Paris" (La mode aime Paris) on the Tower. This light message will stay every night during the Fashion week, from 7pm to 1 am.

News Topics

  1. The Eiffel Tower's Vertical is back in 2016:
    La Verticale de la Tour Eiffel®, an ascent race of the 1,665 steps of the Eiffel Tower is back for its second edition on March 17th, 2016!

  2. Photoquai also on the first level of the Eiffel Tower:
    The Eiffel Tower has joined forces with the Musée du Quai Branly to exhibit, from 22 September to 22 November, previously unseen photography by each of the 40 photographers chosen for the 5th biennial Photoquai.

  3. 14th of July 2015 Fireworks:
    As every year on the occasion of the French national holiday, the city of Paris offers a fireworks display. It will be fired on Monday, July 14th at 11:00 pm from the Eiffel Tower, the Iena bridge and the Trocadero gardens.

  4. A new summer terrace on the 1st floor of the Eiffel Tower:
    From this weekend up to 30 August, visitors to the Eiffel Tower can enjoy the newly renovated first floor summer terrace which is part of the commitment to sustainable development.

  5. Yoga sessions on the Eiffel Tower:
    On June 21st 2015, Lolë, the sports clothing brand from Montreal, is rolling out its famous yellow yoga mats for the Lolë White TourMC 2015, an unforgettable series of yoga events taking place in various cities around the world!
